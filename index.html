Road Racer

Road racer is a racing game where you clear obstacles and kill enemies while collecting coins and aim to gain a high score.

Instructions

  1. Press Space or click on the start game button to start the game.
  2. If you have to pause the game, press P or click on the pause game.
  3. To restart the game, press R or click on the button.
  4. You have three lives and each life contains 3 health, once you lose three health you love one life and after 3 lives game is over.
